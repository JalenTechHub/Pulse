What Pulse helps you do

Security that explains itself.

Focused tools for reviewing suspicious activity—without pretending any automated signal is perfect.

01

Suspicious email detection

Reviews sender, subject, and available preview text for signals associated with phishing, impersonation, urgency, and suspicious behavior.

02

Unified security activity

Brings alerts from the accounts you connect into one organized activity feed.

03

Clear risk explanations

Explains why something may deserve attention instead of only showing a vague warning.

04

User-controlled access

You decide which supported accounts to connect and when to disconnect them.

05

Privacy-focused design

Pulse requests the account permissions needed for the features it currently provides.

06

Resolution tracking

Mark findings as resolved and keep a clearer history of what you have reviewed.

Pulse provides risk signals and review assistance. It does not guarantee that any message is safe or malicious, and it is not a replacement for a professional security product.

How it works

From connection to clarity.

  1. 01

    Connect

    Choose a supported email account you want Pulse to review.

  2. 02

    Authorize

    Pulse securely requests the account permissions required for its current features.

  3. 03

    Analyze

    Sender, subject, and preview signals are analyzed on your iPhone and organized into clear findings.

  4. 04

    Review

    Understand what needs attention, then mark findings as resolved when you are ready.

Pulse provides risk signals and assistance—not absolute guarantees that an email is safe or malicious.

Privacy by intention

Your accounts.
Your decision.

You choose which supported accounts to connect and when to disconnect them. Sender, subject, timestamp, and available preview signals are analyzed on your iPhone—not on a Pulse server.
